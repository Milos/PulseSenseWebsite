Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Pulse Sense stores workout and heart-rate data on your iPhone and can back up workouts to your private iCloud when iCloud is available. The app does not require a Pulse Sense account, does not show ads, and does not include analytics or advertising SDKs.

Who we are

Pulse Sense records heart-rate workouts with compatible Polar H10 chest straps. Pulse Sense is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Polar Electro Oy.

Data processed by the app

Depending on how you use the app, Pulse Sense may process the following information on your device:

  • Workout details, such as workout type, start time, end time, duration, estimated active energy, and training-zone summaries.
  • Heart-rate samples imported from a compatible H10 strap.
  • Bluetooth device information needed to discover, connect to, and reconnect to your selected strap, such as the device name and local identifier.
  • Optional Apple Health profile data, such as age-related information used to personalize heart-rate zones and calorie estimates, if you grant permission.
  • App preferences, such as selected workout types, onboarding completion, appearance, and insight visibility.
  • Private iCloud backup records for saved workouts, if iCloud is available for your Apple ID.

Where your data is stored

Workout data created in Pulse Sense is stored locally on your iPhone. Pulse Sense also uses Apple CloudKit to back up saved workouts to the private iCloud database associated with your Apple ID when iCloud is available.

Pulse Sense does not run its own account system or developer-operated workout server. Your iCloud backup is handled by Apple iCloud infrastructure and is tied to your Apple ID, not to a Pulse Sense account.

Apple Health

If you grant Apple Health permission, Pulse Sense can save imported workouts, heart-rate samples, and active-energy estimates to Apple Health. Pulse Sense may also read limited Apple Health profile information when you allow it, so the app can personalize heart-rate zones and calorie estimates.

Apple Health permissions are controlled by iOS. You can change them at any time in the Health app or iOS Settings. If you deny Health access, Pulse Sense can still store workouts locally, but Health sync and Health-based personalization may not be available.

Bluetooth

Pulse Sense uses Bluetooth to discover and connect to compatible H10 straps. Bluetooth access is used for strap connection, offline recording control, workout import, battery information, and related device status.

iCloud backup

When iCloud is available, Pulse Sense may back up saved workouts to your private iCloud database so your library can be restored after reinstalling the app or moving to another device signed in to the same Apple ID. Backup records may include workout details, heart-rate samples, summaries, calorie estimates, device information, and related sync status.

Tracking, ads, and analytics

Pulse Sense does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies. We do not use your app activity to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Support communications

If you contact support by email, we will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message, screenshots, diagnostics, or details about your device and workout issue. We use this information only to respond to your request and improve support.

This website

This website is a static website intended to provide legal and support information. It does not include advertising or analytics scripts. If hosted on GitHub Pages or another hosting provider, that provider may process standard web server information, such as IP address, browser information, and request logs, according to its own policies.

Data deletion

You can delete local app data by deleting the app from your device. You can manage or delete Apple Health data in the Health app. Private iCloud backup data is associated with your Apple ID and iCloud account. If you want help deleting Pulse Sense iCloud backup data, contact support.

If you delete Health data that Pulse Sense previously saved, Pulse Sense cannot restore it.

Children

Pulse Sense is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes or when legal requirements change. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
